Empanadas

Description

It is a dough filled with meat, vegetables or fruits baked or fried in oil or fat

Ingredients

  • flour
  • butter
  • salt
  • yeast
  • water
  • sugar
  • egg

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add the corn flour, salt, and sugar and mix, then add the water and knead.
  2. When we have a homogeneous dough, we form a ball, cover it with a cloth and make the filling.
  3. Cook the chicken breast in boiling salted water until soft.
  4. When it has lost temperature, shred the chicken meat.
  5. Peel and chop the garlic and onion.
  6. Saut√© the garlic and onion in a pan with oil
  7. Add the shredded chicken breast, cumin, pepper, turmeric, salt and mix. Let it cook for a few minutes, add the tomato sauce and mix.
  8. We make balls with the dough and flatten them on a floured surface.
  9. We place a little of the chicken mixture on top of each empanada.
  10. We fold and flatten the edges well so that the filling does not come out.
  11. We fry the empanadas in plenty of hot oil.
  12. We take them out on kitchen paper to remove excess fat and that's it!
