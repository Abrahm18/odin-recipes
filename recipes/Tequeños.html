Tequeños

Description

They are made with plenty of cheese wrapped in a flaky pastry. They are soft and crunchy at the same time!

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons of Butter
  • piece of Egg Warm water
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper

Steps

  1. The first thing we will do to prepare our dough for tequeños will be to prepare and weigh all the necessary ingredients.
  2. In a bowl or deep container, place the flour along with the egg, butter and salt.
  3. Knead and add warm water little by little to the mixture, until it forms a soft dough that comes off easily from your fingers.
  4. Finally, let the tequeños dough rest for twenty minutes before forming the tequeños. As you will see, it is very easy to prepare this recipe.
